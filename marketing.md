# .claude/rules/marketing.md — Marketing Content Rules

## The 7 Content Types

### 1. Google Business Profile Post
- Length: 150-300 words
- Format: Plain text, no markdown
- CTA: Required — direct to clinic visit or phone call
- Image suggestion: Required (describe image, do not generate)
- Frequency: 2-3x per week max
- UTM: utm_source=sem-27031&utm_medium=gbp&utm_campaign=[campaign-name]

### 2. Instagram Caption
- Length: 100-200 characters (caption) + 3-5 hashtags on separate line
- Tone: Friendly, community-forward, wellness lifestyle focus
- No clinical claims — wellness and lifestyle framing only
- CTA: "Link in bio" or "Visit us at Seminole City Center"
- UTM: utm_source=sem-27031&utm_medium=instagram&utm_campaign=[campaign-name]

### 3. Facebook Post
- Length: 100-250 words
- Format: Conversational, can use line breaks for readability
- CTA: Required — book visit, call, or visit in person
- UTM: utm_source=sem-27031&utm_medium=facebook&utm_campaign=[campaign-name]

### 4. Email Newsletter Segment
- Length: 200-400 words per segment
- Format: Plain text (will be styled in email platform)
- Sections: Greeting, Wellness Tip, Clinic Update, CTA
- Personalization: Use {{first_name}} if segmented list
- Compliance: Must pass full compliance review

### 5. Blog Post / Long-Form Content
- Length: 600-1200 words
- Structure: H1 title, H2 sections, 2-3 internal links, meta description (155 chars max)
- SEO: Must include primary keyword in title, first paragraph, and 2+ H2s
- Tone: Educational, authoritative, wellness-focused
- Compliance: Full review required; all health claims must use approved phrasing

### 6. Promotional Offer Copy
- Length: 50-100 words
- Required elements: Offer details, expiration, CTA, and disclaimer
- Disclaimer: "Offer valid for new patients only at participating locations."
- No clinical outcome promises in offer copy
- UTM: utm_source=sem-27031&utm_medium=[channel]&utm_campaign=promo-[offer-code]

### 7. Community Engagement Content
- Length: 50-150 words
- Format: Event highlights, partner spotlights, local community mentions
- Tone: Local, human, relationship-focused
- No hard sell; no plan pricing

## UTM Schema
All digital content must include UTM parameters:
- utm_source: sem-27031 (always)
- utm_medium: gbp | facebook | instagram | email | sms | referral
- utm_campaign: [descriptive-campaign-name-in-kebab-case]
- utm_content: [optional — specific ad or post variant]

Example: ?utm_source=sem-27031&utm_medium=gbp&utm_campaign=spring-wellness-2026

## Prohibited Marketing Content
- Before/after patient photos (HIPAA and FL scope of practice)
- Specific testimonials with patient names without written consent
- Price comparisons with other chiropractic providers
- Claims about competitor services
- Any content that could be interpreted as medical advertising under FL statute
